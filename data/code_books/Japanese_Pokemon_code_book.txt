Code book of of Japanese_Pokemon.csv

Original article: “Kilpratrick_et_al_2023.pdf”

Variables:

Designation: source of Pokemon name. Official = the official names of Japanese Pokemon; Elicited = elicited names from naive participants.

pokemonname: Pokemon name in Japanese

Output: Pokemon name in transcribed form

Length: Amount of phones in name

Sum of values: Amount of phones in name (as calculated by counting individual segments)

Checksum: assessment of whether Length and Sum of values match

Evolution: Evolution stage of Pokemons: 1 = pre-evolution; 2 = post-evolution, NA = do not evolve

f = amount of /f/ sounds in name

T = amount of /T/ sounds in name

etc. Check paper for details on transcriptions.
